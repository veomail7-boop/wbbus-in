Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the bus information updated?

We update our bus information regularly to ensure accuracy. Route and schedule changes are typically reflected within 24-48 hours of official announcements.

Can I suggest a new bus route to be added?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new routes. Please use the contact form above with the subject "Route Update Request" and provide details about the route you'd like us to include.

Is the information on WBBus.iN free to use?

Yes, all information on WBBus.iN is completely free to access and use. Our goal is to make bus travel information accessible to everyone.

How can I report incorrect information?

If you notice any incorrect information, please contact us immediately using the form above or email us directly. We take accuracy seriously and will investigate and correct any errors promptly.
