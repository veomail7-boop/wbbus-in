The West Bengal State Transport Corporation operates a comprehensive network of bus services connecting major cities, towns, and rural areas across the state. These government-operated buses provide affordable and reliable transportation for millions of passengers daily.

Government Bus Features & Benefits

Affordable Fares

Government buses offer the most economical travel option with subsidized fares for all passengers.

Extensive Network

Comprehensive coverage connecting major cities, towns, and remote villages across West Bengal.

Regular Services

Frequent departures throughout the day ensuring convenient travel options for passengers.

Safety Standards

All government buses undergo regular maintenance and safety checks for passenger security.

Reserved Seating

Special provisions for senior citizens, women, and differently-abled passengers.

Reliable Service

Consistent and dependable service with trained drivers and conductors.

Important Information

Booking & Reservations

Most government buses operate on a first-come, first-served basis. For long-distance routes, advance booking may be available at select bus terminals.

Luggage Policy

Passengers are allowed to carry reasonable personal luggage. Heavy luggage may incur additional charges.

Concessions

Special fare concessions are available for students, senior citizens, and differently-abled passengers with valid identification.
