Booking & Travel Information

How to Book

  • Online booking through operator websites
  • Mobile apps for easy booking
  • Ticket counters at bus terminals
  • Authorized travel agents
  • Phone booking (select operators)

Payment Options

  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • Digital Wallets
  • UPI Payments
  • Cash (at counters)

Cancellation Policy

  • 24+ hours: Full refund (minus service charge)
  • 12-24 hours: 75% refund
  • 6-12 hours: 50% refund
  • Less than 6 hours: 25% refund
  • No-show: No refund

Travel Tips

  • Book in advance for better seats
  • Carry valid ID proof
  • Arrive 30 minutes before departure
  • Keep ticket safe during journey
  • Follow COVID-19 guidelines
